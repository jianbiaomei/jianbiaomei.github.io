Jianbiao Mei

3D Perception | Autonomous Driving

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Control Science and Engineering at Zhejiang University, where I have been since 2021, under the supervision of Prof. Yong Liu at the APRIL Lab. Prior to this, I obtained my B.Eng from the same department with an honor degree at Chu Kochen Honor College in 2021. Currently, I am interning in ADLab at Shanghai AI Laboratory.

Research Interests

The following are my current research interests:

  • Autonomous Driving
  • Emobodied AI
